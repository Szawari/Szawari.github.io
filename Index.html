Our Final Project

We’re building an E-Commerce website focused on computer products (laptops, accessories, and hardware). Our goal is a clean, user-friendly platform where customers can browse, compare, and purchase easily.

The site includes a secure login, product catalog, shopping cart, and smart search & filter. As a team, we split work across front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end (forms, basic data handling).

This project strengthened our teamwork, problem-solving, and web development skills while applying real-world online retail concepts.
